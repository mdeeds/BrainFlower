This page is under active development. Feel free to play around though.
Select two robots. The robot on the left is the robot which you are using to train your net. THe robot on the right is its opponent. Click "collect" a few times to collect training data. Click "train" to apply that training data to the net. You can select the 10x or 100x multipliers to effectively click these buttons a lot of times.
I recommend collecting 10-100 games and training in cycles of 1-10. Training can take a long time
When your robot is trained, you can head over to "play.html" to see it in action. Your net is saved in your browser. You can turn off your computer and come back to it - it will still be there. You can't share your net or check it into the source repository (yet).
